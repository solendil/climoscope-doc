Welcome to Climoscope, a cutting-edge tool designed to provide comprehensive access to historical and near-future climate data. Our platform offers a seamless experience for exploring temperature and precipitation patterns from 1940 to 15 days into the future. Utilizing the robust [Copernicus ERA5 reanalysis](https://cds.climate.copernicus.eu/cdsapp#!/dataset/reanalysis-era5-complete?tab=overview) for historical data and the [GFS weather model](https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/weather-climate-models/global-forecast) for future forecasts, Climoscope enables users to visualize climate change trends, evaluate upcoming weather in relation to seasonal norms, and even replay significant past climatic events. Our intuitive map-based interface, enhanced with color coding and tooltips, ensures that even complex data is easily understandable at a glance.

Climoscope is crafted with user experience in mind, featuring a highly responsive design that sets it apart from other climate tools. The straightforward interface supports [keyboard](./doc/shortcuts.html) shortcuts, allowing for efficient navigation and data manipulation. Whether you are a researcher, educator, or climate enthusiast, Climoscope provides the tools you need to analyze and understand climate data with ease. As we are in active development and beta testing, we greatly value your [feedback](./contact.html) to help us improve and expand our capabilities. Explore the dynamic world of climate data with Climoscope, and join us in our mission to make climate information accessible and actionable for everyone.
